!Question: What do we mean by "level 3 thinking is that the purpose
of testing is to reduce risk"? What risk? Can we reduce the risk to zero?

!Answer:
*** The term above means that whenever a software is used, there will always
be some risk that the software's developer can incur. Therefore, reducing
the risk can increase the quality of the software and improve the values
that the developers will get.
*** Some of the risks are: Inaccurate Estimations, Scope Variations, Poor
Quality Code, Poor Productivity, ...
***
